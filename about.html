About Us

It all started with a dream...

Zenon Hernandez is the founder of Hernandez Landscaping. He came to the United States in hopes for a better life and to chase the American Dream. Through hard work and perseverance he managed to provide landscaping services to homes all over Orange County. The connections built with each homeowner has led Hernandez Landscaping to get an amazing reputation throughout Orange County. Whether it is a clean-up, sprinkler installation or lawn services our clients are satisfied with our work. We focus on the little details such as making sure there is no trash on the street and double-checking the locks on the gate. Our main priority is a high quality of service with an affordable price to keep our customers satisfaction levels above the roof.
